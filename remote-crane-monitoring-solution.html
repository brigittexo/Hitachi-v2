Motor Health Monitoring

The system continuously measures motor voltage and current to assess the health of hoist, cross travel, and long travel motors. By identifying deviations from normal performance, it ensures proactive maintenance and minimizes unexpected failures.

Temperature Monitoring

Integrated temperature sensors track motor heat levels in real-time, alerting operators if temperatures approach unsafe thresholds. This prevents overheating and safeguards against potential component damage.

Vibration Analysis

Advanced Crane Analytics detects subtle irregularities in crane components. Early identification of anomalies helps operators address issues before they escalate into major mechanical faults.

Operational Efficiency Tracking

Operational data from crane movements, such as hoisting, cross travel, and long travel, is collected and analyzed to assess efficiency. This insight helps optimize operations and predict the crane's remaining service life.

Overload and Load Monitoring

Sensors monitor load conditions to ensure cranes operate within prescribed limits. This reduces the risk of overloading and promotes safe handling of heavy materials.

SCADA, VFD, and PLC Monitoring

The monitoring system integrates with SCADA, Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs), and Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) to track control parameters, faults, and system conditions in detail.

GPS Location Tracking

GPS sensors offer real-time tracking of crane locations, ensuring precise positioning and improved logistics management, particularly for mobile and outdoor operations.

Vehicle Speed Monitoring

Speed sensors track crane movement, issuing alerts when speed exceeds predefined safety limits. This feature enhances operational safety in dynamic outdoor environments.

Operational Data Analysis

Sensor data, including operating hours and meter readings, is processed to generate insights about crane utilization. These insights help schedule maintenance and improve equipment usage.
